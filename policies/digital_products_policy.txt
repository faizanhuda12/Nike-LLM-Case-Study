DIGITAL PRODUCTS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Number: CS-POL-009
Version: 1.2

================================================================================
1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy governs digital products including downloadable content, digital 
subscriptions, digital gift cards, and related digital product issues. It 
covers purchase, delivery, access, technical support, and refund procedures 
for digital products. All customer service representatives must understand 
these guidelines to properly assist customers with digital product inquiries.

================================================================================
2. DIGITAL PRODUCT TYPES
================================================================================

2.1 Product Categories
    We offer the following digital products:
    - Digital gift cards
    - Digital subscriptions (training programs, content access)
    - Downloadable content (apps, guides, digital assets)
    - Digital memberships
    - Online course access
    - Digital product keys or licenses

2.2 Digital vs. Physical Products
    Digital products differ from physical products in:
    - Instant delivery (no shipping)
    - No return policy (subject to refund policy exceptions)
    - Access-based rather than ownership-based
    - Platform/device compatibility requirements

================================================================================
3. DIGITAL PRODUCT DELIVERY
================================================================================

3.1 Delivery Method
    Digital products are delivered via:
    - Email with download link or access instructions
    - Account dashboard/portal access
    - In-app or platform activation
    - Digital wallet or account credit (for gift cards)
    
    Delivery is typically immediate upon purchase confirmation.

3.2 Delivery Timeline
    - Standard delivery: Within 15 minutes of purchase confirmation
    - Email delivery: Check spam/junk folders if not received
    - Account access: Available immediately in customer account
    - Platform activation: May require additional setup steps

3.3 Delivery Issues
    If customer reports not receiving digital product:
    - Verify purchase confirmation and payment
    - Check email delivery (spam folder, correct email address)
    - Verify account access and product availability
    - Resend delivery email if needed
    - Provide alternative access method if email delivery failed

================================================================================
4. ACCESS AND ACTIVATION
================================================================================

4.1 Access Requirements
    Digital products may require:
    - Account creation or login
    - Platform-specific accounts (iOS, Android, etc.)
    - Device compatibility
    - Internet connection for activation
    - Email verification

4.2 Activation Process
    - Follow activation instructions provided in delivery email
    - Use provided activation code or link
    - Complete any required account setup
    - Contact support if activation fails after following instructions

4.3 Access Issues
    If customer cannot access digital product:
    - Verify purchase and delivery confirmation
    - Check activation code validity and expiration
    - Verify device/platform compatibility
    - Troubleshoot technical issues
    - Escalate to technical support if access cannot be restored

================================================================================
5. DEVICE AND PLATFORM COMPATIBILITY
================================================================================

5.1 Compatibility Requirements
    Digital products may have specific requirements:
    - Operating system (iOS, Android, Windows, macOS)
    - Device type (smartphone, tablet, computer)
    - Software version requirements
    - Internet connection requirements
    - Account requirements (Apple ID, Google account, etc.)

5.2 Compatibility Information
    - Compatibility requirements stated at time of purchase
    - Customer responsible for verifying compatibility before purchase
    - Refunds available if product incompatible and customer contacts us
      within 48 hours of purchase

5.3 Compatibility Issues
    If product incompatible with customer device:
    - Verify compatibility requirements vs. customer device
    - If our error (incorrect compatibility info): Full refund
    - If customer error (didn't check compatibility): Refund considered
      case-by-case, typically within 48 hours of purchase
    - Provide alternative solutions if available (different platform
      version, etc.)

================================================================================
6. DOWNLOAD AND INSTALLATION ISSUES
================================================================================

6.1 Download Problems
    If customer cannot download product:
    - Verify download link validity and expiration
    - Check file size and storage space requirements
    - Troubleshoot internet connection issues
    - Provide alternative download method if available
    - Resend download link if expired or invalid

6.2 Installation Issues
    Installation problems may be due to:
    - Insufficient device storage
    - Device compatibility issues
    - Software conflicts
    - Corrupted download files
    
    Troubleshooting steps:
    - Verify device meets requirements
    - Check available storage space
    - Try re-downloading
    - Contact technical support for advanced troubleshooting

6.3 Multiple Download Attempts
    - Customers typically allowed 3-5 download attempts
    - Additional attempts may require support assistance
    - Download links may expire after set period (check product terms)
    - Contact support if maximum attempts reached

================================================================================
7. DIGITAL PRODUCT REFUNDS
================================================================================

7.1 General Refund Policy
    Digital products are generally NON-REFUNDABLE due to:
    - Instant delivery and access
    - Inability to "return" digital content
    - Potential for content copying before refund
    
    However, refunds may be available in specific circumstances (Section 7.2).

7.2 Refund Exceptions
    Refunds may be provided for digital products if:
    
    a) Technical Issues (Our Error)
       - Product does not work as described due to our error
       - Activation fails due to system error
       - Compatibility information was incorrect
       - Delivery failed and cannot be resolved
       Refund: Full refund within 30 days of purchase
    
    b) Customer Error (Limited Window)
       - Customer purchased wrong product
       - Customer did not realize purchase (accidental)
       - Customer cannot use product due to incompatibility (if not clearly
         stated at purchase)
       Refund: Full refund if requested within 48 hours of purchase AND
       product not accessed/used
    
    c) Product Not as Described
       - Product does not match description
       - Missing features advertised
       - Quality issues preventing use
       Refund: Full refund within 14 days of purchase
    
    d) Subscription Cancellation
       - Unused portion of subscription period (case-by-case)
       - Cancellation within cancellation window per subscription terms

7.3 Refund Process
    For approved refunds:
    - Refund processed within 1 business day
    - Refund to original payment method (5-10 business days)
    - Access to product revoked upon refund
    - Customer notified of refund and access revocation

7.4 Non-Refundable Situations
    Refunds are NOT available for:
    - Change of mind after accessing product
    - Product used or accessed beyond refund window
    - Compatibility issues if requirements clearly stated
    - Customer error if product already accessed
    - Expired subscriptions (no refund for unused time)

================================================================================
8. DIGITAL GIFT CARDS
================================================================================

8.1 Gift Card Delivery
    - Digital gift cards delivered via email within 15 minutes
    - Gift card code or link provided in email
    - Gift card added to recipient's account if account exists
    - Gift card can be forwarded to recipient if purchased as gift

8.2 Gift Card Usage
    - Gift cards can be used for purchases on our website
    - Gift cards do not expire (check regional regulations)
    - Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash
    - Gift cards can be combined with other payment methods
    - Partial balance remains if purchase less than gift card value

8.3 Gift Card Issues
    If gift card not working:
    - Verify gift card code entered correctly
    - Check gift card balance and validity
    - Verify gift card not already used
    - Check expiration date (if applicable)
    - Contact support if issue persists

8.4 Gift Card Refunds
    - Gift cards are NON-REFUNDABLE once delivered
    - Cannot return or refund digital gift cards
    - Gift card purchases cannot be cancelled after delivery

================================================================================
9. SUBSCRIPTIONS AND MEMBERSHIPS
================================================================================

9.1 Subscription Terms
    Digital subscriptions:
    - Auto-renew unless cancelled
    - Billing cycle per subscription terms (monthly, annual, etc.)
    - Cancellation available anytime
    - Access continues until end of current billing period

9.2 Subscription Cancellation
    - Cancel anytime through account settings or customer service
    - Cancellation effective at end of current billing period
    - No refund for current period (access continues until period ends)
    - Must cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to prevent next charge

9.3 Subscription Refunds
    - No refund for current billing period after cancellation
    - Unused portion refunds: Case-by-case, requires supervisor approval
    - Refunds typically only for technical issues or our errors
    - Annual subscriptions: May prorate refund if cancelled early (check
      subscription terms)

================================================================================
10. TECHNICAL SUPPORT
================================================================================

10.1 Support Scope
    We provide technical support for:
    - Delivery and access issues
    - Activation problems
    - Compatibility questions
    - Download and installation help
    - Account access issues
    
    We do NOT provide:
    - General device troubleshooting
    - Third-party software support
    - Network configuration help
    - Advanced technical customization

10.2 Support Process
    - Initial troubleshooting via customer service
    - Escalate to technical specialist if needed
    - Provide step-by-step guidance
    - Document issues for product improvement
    - Follow up to ensure resolution

10.3 Escalation to Technical Team
    Escalate to technical specialist for:
    - Complex activation issues
    - Platform-specific problems
    - Account access cannot be restored
    - Product functionality issues
    - Multiple failed download attempts

================================================================================
11. ACCOUNT AND ACCESS MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

11.1 Account Requirements
    Some digital products require:
    - Customer account creation
    - Email verification
    - Platform account (Apple ID, Google account, etc.)
    - Account linking for access

11.2 Access Recovery
    If customer cannot access account:
    - Verify account ownership
    - Reset password if needed
    - Verify email address
    - Restore access to digital products
    - Link products to correct account if needed

11.3 Multiple Device Access
    - Digital products may allow access on multiple devices
    - Check product terms for device limit
    - Some products limited to single device
    - Account sharing may violate terms of service

================================================================================
12. ESCALATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

Escalate digital product issues to supervisor or specialist for:
- Refund requests outside standard policy
- Technical issues preventing product use
- Account access cannot be restored
- Compatibility disputes
- Subscription refund requests
- High-value digital product issues
- Suspected fraud or unauthorized access
- Product functionality complaints

================================================================================
13. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

All digital product interactions must include:
- Product type and purchase details
- Delivery method and confirmation
- Access or technical issues reported
- Troubleshooting steps attempted
- Resolution or refund processing
- Follow-up requirements
