LOYALTY POINTS AND REWARDS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Number: CS-POL-008
Version: 1.4

================================================================================
1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy governs our loyalty program, including points earning, points 
redemption, points posting timelines, tier benefits, and points-related 
inquiries. All customer service representatives must understand these 
guidelines to properly assist customers with loyalty program questions and 
issues.

================================================================================
2. LOYALTY PROGRAM STRUCTURE
================================================================================

2.1 Membership Tiers
    Our loyalty program has four tiers:
    
    - Guest: No membership, no automatic benefits
    - Member: Free membership, 5% automatic discount, points earning
    - VIP: Enhanced membership, 10% automatic discount, bonus points,
      exclusive promotions
    - Employee: Special employee tier with employee-specific benefits
    
    Tier benefits are cumulative (VIP includes all Member benefits plus
    additional VIP benefits).

2.2 Points Earning Rate
    Points are earned on qualifying purchases:
    - Members: 1 point per $1 spent (1% back in points)
    - VIP: 1.5 points per $1 spent (1.5% back in points)
    - Employees: Per employee policy (confidential)
    - Guests: No points earned (must join program)

2.3 Qualifying Purchases
    Points are earned on:
    - Product purchases (full price, sale, clearance)
    - Gift card purchases (points earned on purchase, not redemption)
    
    Points are NOT earned on:
    - Shipping fees
    - Taxes
    - Returns and refunds (points deducted if item returned)
    - Using points for purchase (no points on points)

================================================================================
3. POINTS POSTING TIMELINE
================================================================================

3.1 Standard Points Posting
    Points are typically posted to customer accounts:
    - After order ships: Points post within 24-48 hours of shipment
    - For in-store purchases: Points post within 24-48 hours of purchase
    - For online purchases: Points post after order fulfillment
    
    Standard posting window: 1-4 business days after order ships

3.2 Points Posting Delays
    Points may be delayed due to:
    - Order processing delays
    - System processing issues
    - Account verification requirements
    - High order volume periods
    
    If points not posted within 7 business days of shipment, investigate
    and escalate if needed.

3.3 Points for Pending Orders
    - Points do NOT post for pending or unfulfilled orders
    - Points post only after order ships
    - Cancelled orders do not earn points
    - If order cancelled after points posted, points will be deducted

================================================================================
4. POINTS REDEMPTION
================================================================================

4.1 Redemption Value
    Points can be redeemed for:
    - Discount on future purchases: 100 points = $1.00 discount
    - Minimum redemption: 500 points ($5.00 discount)
    - Maximum redemption per order: Varies by tier
      * Member: Up to $50 per order (5,000 points)
      * VIP: Up to $100 per order (10,000 points)
      * Employee: Per employee policy

4.2 Redemption Process
    Customers can redeem points:
    - At checkout (online or in-store)
    - Through customer service (for adjustments)
    - Points applied as discount to order total
    - Points deducted from account immediately upon redemption

4.3 Redemption Restrictions
    Points cannot be redeemed for:
    - Gift card purchases
    - Shipping fees (in most cases)
    - Taxes
    - Certain restricted items (check item eligibility)
    - Combined with some promotional codes (check promotion terms)

4.4 Points Expiration
    - Points do NOT expire for active accounts
    - Points expire if account inactive for 24 months (no purchases)
    - Expired points cannot be reinstated
    - Customers notified before points expiration (if contact info current)

================================================================================
5. POINTS ADJUSTMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 Returns and Points Deduction
    When customer returns item:
    - Points earned on returned item are deducted from account
    - Deduction occurs when return is processed
    - If customer used points for purchase and returns item:
      * Points used for purchase are refunded
      * Points earned on purchase are deducted
      * Net points adjustment calculated

5.2 Price Adjustments
    If order price adjusted (price match, promotion adjustment):
    - Points recalculated based on final price
    - Points adjusted within 3-5 business days
    - Customer notified of points adjustment

5.3 Points Correction
    If points incorrectly posted or deducted:
    - Investigate and verify correct points amount
    - Adjust points account accordingly
    - Document correction reason
    - Notify customer of correction

5.4 Missing Points
    If customer reports missing points:
    - Verify order shipped and points should have posted
    - Check points posting timeline (may still be processing)
    - If points should have posted but haven't: Manually add points
    - Document issue for system review if pattern detected

================================================================================
6. TIER BENEFITS AND UPGRADES
================================================================================

6.1 Tier Qualification
    - Member: Free to join, automatic 5% discount
    - VIP: Qualify by spending $500+ in previous 12 months OR maintain
      active membership with consistent purchases
    - Tier status reviewed annually
    - Tier downgrades: If VIP qualifications not met, may downgrade to
      Member (notification provided)

6.2 Tier Benefits Summary
    Member Benefits:
    - 5% automatic discount on all purchases
    - Points earning (1 point per $1)
    - Early access to sales (24 hours before public)
    - Free standard shipping on orders $50+ (select regions)
    
    VIP Benefits (includes all Member benefits plus):
    - 10% automatic discount on all purchases
    - Enhanced points earning (1.5 points per $1)
    - Exclusive VIP-only promotions
    - Free expedited shipping on orders $100+ (select regions)
    - Birthday rewards
    - Dedicated customer service support

6.3 Tier Upgrades
    - Automatic upgrade when qualifications met
    - Upgrade effective immediately upon qualification
    - Benefits apply to next purchase
    - Customer notified of tier upgrade

6.4 Tier Downgrades
    - Annual review of tier qualifications
    - If VIP qualifications not met: Downgrade to Member
    - 30-day notice before downgrade
    - Points and benefits continue at new tier level
    - Opportunity to requalify for VIP within notice period

================================================================================
7. POINTS TRANSFER AND GIFTING
================================================================================

7.1 Points Transfer Policy
    - Points are NON-TRANSFERABLE between accounts
    - Points cannot be gifted or transferred to other customers
    - Points are tied to specific customer account
    - Family accounts: Each family member has separate account and points

7.2 Account Merging
    If customer has multiple accounts and wants to merge:
    - Points from both accounts can be combined
    - Requires account verification
    - One-time account merge process
    - Escalate to account specialist for processing

================================================================================
8. POINTS DISPUTES AND CORRECTIONS
================================================================================

8.1 Points Dispute Process
    When customer disputes points:
    - Review order history and points posting records
    - Verify points should have been earned
    - Check for system errors or posting delays
    - If error confirmed: Correct points immediately
    - If points correct: Explain points calculation clearly

8.2 Points Correction Timeline
    - Points corrections processed within 1-2 business days
    - Customer notified of correction
    - Points appear in account immediately after correction

8.3 Points System Errors
    If system error caused points issue:
    - Correct points immediately
    - Document error for technical team
    - Provide compensation if significant error (discount code, etc.)
    - Monitor for pattern of errors

================================================================================
9. SPECIAL PROMOTIONS AND BONUS POINTS
================================================================================

9.1 Bonus Points Promotions
    Periodically, we offer bonus points promotions:
    - Double points days/weeks
    - Bonus points on specific categories
    - Tier-specific bonus point offers
    - Promotional bonus points terms clearly stated

9.2 Bonus Points Posting
    - Bonus points post with standard points
    - Bonus points subject to same posting timeline
    - Bonus points can be redeemed like standard points
    - Bonus points may have expiration (check promotion terms)

9.3 Promotional Points Restrictions
    - Bonus points promotions may have restrictions
    - Check promotion terms for eligibility
    - Some promotions exclude clearance or sale items
    - Promotional points may not stack with other offers

================================================================================
10. ACCOUNT ISSUES AND POINTS
================================================================================

10.1 Account Verification
    If account requires verification:
    - Points posting may be delayed until verification complete
    - Verify account to restore normal points processing
    - Points will post retroactively once verified

10.2 Account Closure
    If customer account closed:
    - Points are forfeited
    - Points cannot be transferred or refunded
    - Customer notified before account closure if points balance exists

10.3 Inactive Accounts
    - Accounts inactive 24+ months: Points expire
    - Reactivate account: Expired points cannot be restored
    - Make purchase to reactivate account and prevent future expiration

================================================================================
11. ESCALATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

Escalate loyalty points issues to supervisor or specialist for:
- Points disputes over 1,000 points
- Account merging requests
- Tier qualification disputes
- System errors affecting multiple customers
- Complex points adjustments (returns with points used)
- Points expiration disputes
- VIP tier downgrade appeals
- Suspected points fraud or abuse

================================================================================
12. COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES
================================================================================

When communicating about loyalty points:
- Clearly explain points earning and redemption
- Show points balance and recent activity
- Explain tier benefits and qualifications
- Set expectations for points posting timelines
- Provide clear next steps for points issues
- Show empathy for points-related frustrations

================================================================================
13. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

All loyalty points interactions must include:
- Customer account and tier level
- Points balance before and after transaction
- Points earned or redeemed amount
- Order numbers linked to points transactions
- Points posting or adjustment dates
- Resolution actions taken
- Follow-up requirements if applicable
