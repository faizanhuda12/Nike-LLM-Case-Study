SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Number: CS-POL-002
Version: 1.8

================================================================================
1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy establishes standards for shipping, delivery, and handling of 
delivery-related customer inquiries. It covers standard shipping, expedited 
options, delivery delays, address issues, and compensation for service failures.

================================================================================
2. SHIPPING OPTIONS AND TIMEFRAMES
================================================================================

2.1 Standard Shipping
    - Processing Time: 1-2 business days after order confirmation
    - Delivery Time: 5-7 business days from shipment
    - Available to: All regions
    - Tracking: Provided via email upon shipment

2.2 Expedited Shipping
    - Processing Time: 1 business day after order confirmation
    - Delivery Time: 2-3 business days from shipment
    - Available to: Select regions (check regional availability)
    - Additional fees apply

2.3 Express Shipping
    - Processing Time: Same day (orders placed before 2 PM local time)
    - Delivery Time: 1-2 business days from shipment
    - Available to: Major metropolitan areas only
    - Additional fees apply

2.4 International Shipping
    - Processing Time: 2-3 business days after order confirmation
    - Delivery Time: 10-21 business days depending on destination
    - Customs and duties: Customer responsibility
    - Tracking: Provided where available

================================================================================
3. DELIVERY DELAYS AND COMPENSATION
================================================================================

3.1 Delay Thresholds
    Delivery delays are categorized as follows:
    
    - Minor Delay: 1-3 business days beyond estimated delivery
      Action: Monitor and provide tracking updates
      Compensation: None (within carrier variance)
    
    - Moderate Delay: 4-6 business days beyond estimated delivery
      Action: Proactive customer communication, expedited resolution
      Compensation: Consider shipping fee refund or discount code
    
    - Significant Delay: 7+ business days beyond estimated delivery
      Action: Immediate escalation, full investigation
      Compensation: Shipping fee refund, discount code, or expedited 
                    replacement at no cost

3.2 Weather-Related Delays
    Delays caused by severe weather conditions, natural disasters, or 
    carrier-acknowledged weather events:
    - Customer will be notified of delay
    - No compensation required for weather-related delays
    - Delivery will proceed once conditions permit
    - If delay exceeds 14 days, customer may request cancellation and 
      full refund

3.3 Carrier Issues
    Delays caused by carrier errors, lost packages, or carrier system failures:
    - Immediate investigation with carrier
    - Customer notification within 24 hours
    - Compensation per Section 3.1 based on delay severity
    - Replacement shipment expedited at no cost if package confirmed lost

3.4 Our Error Delays
    Delays caused by our fulfillment errors, inventory issues, or processing 
    mistakes:
    - Immediate acknowledgment and apology
    - Expedited shipment at no additional cost
    - Full shipping fee refund
    - Discount code for inconvenience (minimum 10% off next purchase)

================================================================================
4. ADDRESS ISSUES
================================================================================

4.1 Incorrect Address Provided by Customer
    If customer provided incorrect address:
    - Customer responsible for any additional shipping costs
    - Package may be returned to sender if undeliverable
    - Customer may request reshipment to correct address (additional shipping
      fees apply)
    - No compensation provided

4.2 Address Mismatch (System Error)
    If address error is due to our system or processing error:
    - We cover all reshipment costs
    - Expedited shipping provided at no cost
    - Full refund of original shipping if package is lost or significantly
      delayed due to error

4.3 Carrier Address Exception
    If carrier cannot deliver due to address issues (not customer error):
    - Customer will be notified via email and/or phone
    - Customer has 7 days to provide corrected address
    - After 7 days, package may be returned to sender
    - Reshipment fees may apply depending on circumstances

4.4 Address Updates After Shipment
    Address changes requested after order has shipped:
    - Cannot be guaranteed
    - Customer may contact carrier directly if within carrier's redirect window
    - We will attempt to assist but cannot guarantee success
    - No compensation if address change fails

================================================================================
5. MISSING OR DAMAGED IN TRANSIT ITEMS
================================================================================

5.1 Missing Items
    If package is marked delivered but customer reports not received:
    - Verify delivery address matches order
    - Check carrier delivery confirmation and signature (if applicable)
    - If confirmed delivered to correct address: Escalate to specialized team
    - If delivery cannot be confirmed: Process replacement or refund
    
    Investigation timeline: 3-5 business days
    Resolution: Replacement shipped or full refund issued

5.2 Damaged in Transit
    Items damaged during shipping:
    - Customer must report within 48 hours of delivery
    - Photographs of damage and packaging required
    - Immediate replacement or full refund
    - Return shipping label provided for damaged item
    - Expedited replacement at no cost

5.3 Package Not Received (Lost in Transit)
    If package tracking shows no movement for 7+ business days:
    - Initiate carrier investigation
    - Customer notification within 24 hours
    - If carrier confirms loss: Immediate replacement or full refund
    - If carrier investigation pending: Provide timeline and keep customer
      informed every 2-3 business days

================================================================================
6. TRACKING AND NOTIFICATIONS
================================================================================

6.1 Tracking Information
    - Tracking number provided via email upon shipment
    - Customers can track orders through our website or carrier website
    - Automated email updates at key delivery milestones
    - SMS notifications available (opt-in required)

6.2 Delivery Confirmation
    - Signature required for orders over $200 (select regions)
    - Photo proof of delivery available in select areas
    - Delivery confirmation email sent upon delivery

================================================================================
7. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING AND CUSTOMS
================================================================================

7.1 Customs and Duties
    - Customer is responsible for all customs fees, duties, and taxes
    - Estimated duties may be calculated at checkout (where available)
    - Final duty amount determined by destination country customs
    - We are not responsible for customs delays or additional fees

7.2 International Delivery Delays
    International shipping delays due to customs:
    - Customer will be notified if we receive customs hold notification
    - Customer responsible for providing required customs documentation
    - No compensation for customs-related delays
    - If package held more than 30 days, customer may request cancellation

================================================================================
8. STORE PICKUP ORDERS
================================================================================

8.1 Pickup Window
    - Orders available for pickup within 2-4 hours of order confirmation
      (during store hours)
    - Customer has 7 calendar days to pick up order
    - After 7 days, order may be cancelled and refunded

8.2 Pickup Issues
    - If item not available at selected store: Customer notified, option to
      wait for restock, transfer to another store, or full refund
    - If pickup window expired: Customer may request extension (one-time,
      up to 3 additional days) or cancellation with full refund
    - Store stock issues: Immediate notification, alternative options provided

================================================================================
9. PREORDER DELAYS
================================================================================

9.1 Preorder Estimated Dates
    - Estimated delivery dates provided at time of preorder
    - Dates are estimates and subject to supplier/manufacturer timelines
    - Customers notified of any changes to estimated delivery dates

9.2 Preorder Delay Compensation
    - Delays up to 7 days: No compensation (within normal variance)
    - Delays 8-14 days: 10% discount code for inconvenience
    - Delays 15-30 days: 15% discount code or option to cancel with full refund
    - Delays over 30 days: 20% discount code or immediate cancellation with
      full refund upon request

9.3 Supplier Issues
    If preorder delay is due to supplier/manufacturer issues:
    - Transparent communication to customers
    - Regular updates (minimum weekly) until new delivery date confirmed
    - Compensation per Section 9.2
    - Option to cancel at any time with full refund

================================================================================
10. ESCALATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

Escalate shipping and delivery issues to supervisor or specialized team for:
- Delays exceeding 14 business days
- Multiple packages lost for same customer
- High-value orders ($500+) with delivery issues
- International orders with customs complications
- Suspected fraudulent delivery claims
- Carrier disputes requiring legal or contract review
- Weather-related delays exceeding 14 days

================================================================================
11. COMPENSATION GUIDELINES
================================================================================

11.1 Shipping Fee Refunds
    - Full refund of shipping fees for delays 7+ days (our error)
    - Partial refund consideration for delays 4-6 days
    - No refund for delays 1-3 days (carrier variance)

11.2 Discount Codes
    - 10% off: Moderate delays (4-6 days) or minor inconveniences
    - 15% off: Significant delays (7-14 days)
    - 20% off: Extended delays (15+ days) or multiple issues

11.3 Expedited Replacement
    - Provided at no cost for: Lost packages, damaged items, our errors
    - Next-day or 2-day shipping when available
    - Standard shipping if expedited not available in region

================================================================================
12. DOCUMENTATION AND COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

All shipping and delivery interactions must be:
- Documented with order number and tracking information
- Tagged with appropriate issue type and resolution code
- Retained per regional data retention requirements
- Linked to customer account for pattern analysis
