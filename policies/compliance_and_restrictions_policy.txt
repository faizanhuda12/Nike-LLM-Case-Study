COMPLIANCE AND RESTRICTIONS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Number: CS-POL-011
Version: 1.2

================================================================================
1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy governs product restrictions, regional compliance requirements, 
age restrictions, export controls, and regulatory compliance issues. It 
provides guidance for handling customer inquiries about restricted products, 
shipping restrictions, and compliance-related questions.

================================================================================
2. REGIONAL SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS
================================================================================

2.1 Restricted Regions
    Some products may not be available for shipment to certain regions due to:
    - Regulatory restrictions
    - Export controls
    - Product-specific regulations
    - Carrier limitations
    - Business decisions
    
    Restricted regions and products are identified in our system and at
    checkout.

2.2 Restricted Product Categories
    Certain product categories may have regional restrictions:
    - Electronics with specific certifications
    - Products with battery restrictions
    - Items subject to import regulations
    - Products requiring special licenses
    - Items restricted by destination country laws

2.3 Handling Restricted Orders
    If customer attempts to order restricted product:
    - System prevents order completion if restriction detected
    - Customer notified of restriction at checkout
    - Alternative products suggested if available
    - Explanation provided for restriction when possible
    - Customer service can clarify restrictions but cannot override

2.4 Regional Compliance Requirements
    Orders must comply with:
    - Destination country import regulations
    - Product-specific regulations (safety, certification, etc.)
    - Export control laws (if applicable)
    - Regional consumer protection laws
    - Customs and trade regulations

================================================================================
3. AGE RESTRICTIONS AND VERIFICATION
================================================================================

3.1 Age-Restricted Products
    Some products have age restrictions:
    - Products requiring minimum age (18+, 21+, etc.)
    - Age verification required at purchase
    - Age verification may be required at delivery
    - Restrictions based on product type and regional laws

3.2 Age Verification Process
    Age verification may require:
    - Account age verification during registration
    - ID verification at checkout for age-restricted items
    - Signature and ID verification at delivery
    - Third-party age verification services
    
    Age verification requirements vary by product and region.

3.3 Age Verification Failure
    If age verification fails:
    - Order cannot be completed
    - Customer notified of verification requirement
    - Alternative verification methods offered if available
    - Refund provided if order placed but verification fails

3.4 Age Restriction Inquiries
    When customers ask about age restrictions:
    - Clearly state age requirement
    - Explain why restriction exists (legal requirement, product safety, etc.)
    - Provide information about verification process
    - Suggest alternative products if available

================================================================================
4. EXPORT CONTROLS
================================================================================

4.1 Export-Controlled Products
    Some products may be subject to export control regulations:
    - Products with dual-use technology
    - Items restricted by export control laws
    - Products requiring export licenses
    - Items restricted to certain countries
    
    Export controls are legal requirements we must comply with.

4.2 Export Control Compliance
    - We screen orders for export control compliance
    - Restricted destinations identified automatically
    - Orders to restricted destinations cannot be processed
    - Customer notified if order restricted by export controls

4.3 Export Control Inquiries
    If customer asks about export restrictions:
    - Explain that restrictions are legal requirements
    - Cannot override export control restrictions
    - Suggest alternative products if available
    - Provide general information but cannot provide legal advice

================================================================================
5. PRODUCT-SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS
================================================================================

5.1 Battery and Electronics Restrictions
    Products with batteries may have restrictions:
    - Lithium battery shipping restrictions
    - Air shipping limitations for certain battery types
    - Regional battery regulations
    - Carrier-specific battery restrictions
    
    Restrictions displayed at checkout and in product details.

5.2 Size and Weight Restrictions
    Some products have shipping restrictions due to:
    - Oversized items (carrier limitations)
    - Weight restrictions (carrier or regional limits)
    - Fragile item handling requirements
    - Special shipping requirements
    
    Alternative shipping methods may be available.

5.3 Hazardous Materials
    Products classified as hazardous materials:
    - Subject to special shipping requirements
    - May have restricted shipping destinations
    - Require special packaging and labeling
    - May require additional shipping fees
    
    Hazardous material restrictions are safety and legal requirements.

================================================================================
6. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

6.1 Product Safety and Certification
    Products must meet:
    - Destination country safety standards
    - Required certifications (CE, FCC, etc.)
    - Regional product regulations
    - Industry-specific standards
    
    Non-compliant products cannot be shipped to regions requiring compliance.

6.2 Labeling and Documentation Requirements
    Some regions require:
    - Specific product labeling
    - Language requirements for labels
    - Regulatory documentation
    - Compliance certificates
    
    We provide required documentation where applicable.

6.3 Compliance Inquiries
    When customers ask about compliance:
    - Explain compliance requirements when known
    - Provide product certification information if available
    - Refer to product details and specifications
    - Escalate complex compliance questions to specialist

================================================================================
7. RESTRICTED CUSTOMER ACCOUNTS
================================================================================

7.1 Account Restrictions
    Customer accounts may be restricted due to:
    - Violation of terms of service
    - Suspected fraudulent activity
    - Payment issues
    - Return policy abuse
    - Other policy violations
    
    Restricted accounts have limited functionality.

7.2 Restriction Notification
    - Customers notified of account restrictions
    - Reason for restriction explained (when appropriate)
    - Steps to resolve restriction provided
    - Appeal process available for certain restrictions

7.3 Restriction Resolution
    To resolve account restrictions:
    - Address underlying issue (payment, return, etc.)
    - Contact customer service for review
    - Provide required documentation
    - Follow resolution process provided

================================================================================
8. INTERNATIONAL TRADE RESTRICTIONS
================================================================================

8.1 Trade Embargoes and Sanctions
    We comply with:
    - International trade embargoes
    - Economic sanctions
    - Trade restrictions by government authorities
    - Export ban lists
    
    Orders to embargoed or sanctioned regions cannot be processed.

8.2 Sanctioned Regions
    - Orders to sanctioned regions automatically blocked
    - Customer notified if order restricted by sanctions
    - Cannot override sanction restrictions
    - Restrictions are legal requirements

8.3 Trade Restriction Inquiries
    If customer asks about trade restrictions:
    - Explain that restrictions are legal requirements
    - Cannot provide legal advice on trade restrictions
    - Suggest consulting with trade compliance specialist if needed
    - Provide general information only

================================================================================
9. PRODUCT RECALLS AND SAFETY NOTICES
================================================================================

9.1 Recall Notifications
    If product subject to recall:
    - Customers notified immediately
    - Recall information provided
    - Return/refund process for recalled items
    - Safety instructions provided if applicable

9.2 Safety Notices
    Safety notices issued for:
    - Product safety concerns
    - Usage warnings
    - Important safety information
    - Regulatory updates affecting product use
    
    Customers notified and provided with safety information.

9.3 Recall and Safety Inquiries
    When customers ask about recalls or safety:
    - Check for active recalls or safety notices
    - Provide recall or safety information
    - Assist with return/refund if recalled
    - Escalate to product safety team if needed

================================================================================
10. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

10.1 Regional Data Protection
    We comply with regional data protection regulations:
    - GDPR (European Union)
    - CCPA (California)
    - Other regional privacy laws
    - Data retention requirements
    
    Customer data handled per privacy policy and regulations.

10.2 Privacy Inquiries
    When customers ask about privacy:
    - Refer to privacy policy
    - Explain data handling practices
    - Assist with privacy requests (data access, deletion, etc.)
    - Escalate complex privacy questions to privacy team

================================================================================
11. ACCESSIBILITY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

11.1 Accessibility Requirements
    We strive to comply with:
    - Web accessibility standards (WCAG)
    - Regional accessibility requirements
    - Product accessibility standards where applicable
    
    Accessibility is important for inclusive customer experience.

11.2 Accessibility Inquiries
    When customers ask about accessibility:
    - Provide information about accessibility features
    - Assist with accessibility needs
    - Escalate accessibility concerns to appropriate team
    - Document accessibility feedback for improvement

================================================================================
12. ESCALATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

Escalate compliance and restriction issues to supervisor or specialist for:
- Complex regulatory compliance questions
- Export control inquiries requiring legal review
- Age verification disputes
- Account restriction appeals
- Product recall or safety concerns
- Trade restriction questions
- Regional compliance law questions
- Accessibility compliance issues
- Data protection and privacy requests
- Any restriction override requests (typically cannot override)

================================================================================
13. COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES
================================================================================

When communicating about restrictions and compliance:
- Clearly explain why restriction exists (legal requirement, safety, etc.)
- Show empathy for customer frustration
- Provide alternatives when available
- Explain that restrictions are requirements, not preferences
- Maintain professional tone even when restriction cannot be overridden
- Provide clear next steps when possible

================================================================================
14. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

All compliance and restriction interactions must include:
- Restriction type and reason
- Product or region affected
- Customer inquiry and response
- Alternatives suggested (if any)
- Escalation notes if applicable
- Follow-up requirements

================================================================================
END OF POLICY
