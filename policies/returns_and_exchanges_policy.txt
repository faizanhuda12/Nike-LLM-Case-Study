RETURNS AND EXCHANGES POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Number: CS-POL-001
Version: 2.1

================================================================================
1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy governs all returns and exchanges for products purchased through our 
online, mobile, and retail channels. All customer service representatives must 
adhere strictly to these guidelines to ensure consistent application of our 
return and exchange procedures across all regions and customer tiers.

================================================================================
2. RETURN WINDOW AND ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

2.1 Standard Return Window
    Returns are accepted within the following timeframes from the date of 
    delivery, as confirmed by carrier tracking:
    
    - United States (US): 60 calendar days
    - Canada (CA): 60 calendar days
    - European Union (EU): 30 calendar days
    - United Kingdom (UK): 30 calendar days
    - Australia (AU): 45 calendar days
    
    The return window begins on the date of delivery, not the date of purchase.

2.2 Eligible Items for Return
    Items are eligible for return if they meet ALL of the following criteria:
    
    a) Returned within the applicable return window (Section 2.1)
    b) In original, unworn condition with all original tags attached
    c) In original packaging when applicable
    d) Not classified as final sale, clearance, or special promotion items
      (unless explicitly stated otherwise in the promotion terms)
    
2.3 Condition Requirements
    Items must be in one of the following acceptable conditions:
    
    - Unworn: Item has never been worn or used, with all tags and packaging intact
    - Tried Indoors: Item was tried on indoors only, shows no signs of wear,
      and all tags remain attached
    
    Items in the following conditions are NOT eligible for standard returns:
    - Worn Outdoors: Item shows signs of outdoor use or wear
    - Tags Removed: Original tags have been removed or detached
    - Damaged by Customer: Item shows damage not present at time of delivery
    
2.4 Defective Items Exception
    Items with manufacturing defects are ALWAYS eligible for return or exchange,
    regardless of:
    - Time since delivery (no time limit)
    - Condition of packaging
    - Presence of original tags
    
    Manufacturing defects include but are not limited to:
    - Material defects (fabric tears, seam failures, sole separation)
    - Incorrect sizing labels (item does not match labeled size)
    - Missing components (missing laces, buttons, zippers)
    - Color defects (bleeding, fading, incorrect dye application)
    
    All defective item claims require verification. Customer service 
    representatives must document the defect description and may request 
    photographs for verification purposes.

================================================================================
3. EXCHANGE POLICY
================================================================================

3.1 Exchange Eligibility
    Exchanges follow the same eligibility criteria as returns (Section 2).
    Exchanges are available for:
    - Size exchanges (subject to availability)
    - Color exchanges (subject to availability)
    - Style exchanges (subject to availability and price difference)
    
3.2 Exchange Process
    a) Customer must initiate exchange within the return window
    b) Original item must meet return condition requirements
    c) New item must be of equal or greater value, or customer pays difference
    d) If new item is of lesser value, refund for difference will be processed
       after return is received and verified

================================================================================
4. REFUND PROCESSING
================================================================================

4.1 Refund Method
    Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.
    Processing time: 5-10 business days after we receive and verify the 
    returned item.

4.2 Refund Amount
    - Full refund: Original purchase price (excluding original shipping fees
      unless return is due to our error)
    - Partial refund: May apply if item is returned in condition that does not
      meet full return criteria but qualifies for partial credit (requires
      supervisor approval)

4.3 Shipping Costs
    - Standard returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
    - Defective items: We provide prepaid return shipping label
    - Our error returns: We provide prepaid return shipping label and refund
      original shipping costs

================================================================================
5. FINAL SALE ITEMS
================================================================================

The following items are FINAL SALE and not eligible for return or exchange:
- Clearance items (unless defective)
- Custom or personalized items
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Gift cards and digital products
- Items purchased with certain promotional codes (as specified in promotion)

================================================================================
6. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES
================================================================================

6.1 Holiday Returns
    Items purchased between November 1 and December 24 are eligible for 
    extended return window until January 31 of the following year, subject to
    standard condition requirements.

6.2 Gift Returns
    Gift returns are accepted within the standard return window. Gift recipients
    will receive store credit or exchange for the current selling price of the
    item at time of return.

6.3 VIP and Employee Returns
    VIP members and employees may be eligible for extended return windows or
    additional flexibility. Refer to tier-specific policy addendums.

================================================================================
7. RETURN PROCESSING
================================================================================

7.1 Return Authorization
    All returns require a return authorization (RA) number. Customers must
    initiate return through:
    - Online portal
    - Customer service chat
    - Email support
    - Phone support
    
    RA number must be included with returned package.

7.2 Return Inspection
    Upon receipt, all returned items undergo inspection to verify:
    - Condition matches return reason stated
    - Item matches order details
    - All components are present
    - No signs of wear or damage beyond acceptable limits
    
    Items failing inspection will be returned to customer or processed as
    partial refund (requires supervisor approval).

================================================================================
8. ESCALATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

The following situations require escalation to a supervisor or specialized team:
- Returns outside standard window with extenuating circumstances
- High-value items (over $500)
- Disputes regarding item condition assessment
- Multiple returns from same customer within short timeframe
- Suspected fraudulent return activity
- Returns involving gift cards or store credit complications

================================================================================
9. COMPLIANCE AND DOCUMENTATION
================================================================================

All return and exchange transactions must be:
- Documented in customer service system
- Tagged with appropriate return reason code
- Linked to original order number
- Retained in records per regional data retention requirements

================================================================================
10. POLICY UPDATES
================================================================================

This policy is subject to periodic review and updates. Customer service
representatives will be notified of policy changes through official channels.
When policy changes affect active return windows, customers will be notified
at time of purchase or through email communication.

================================================================================
END OF POLICY
